OUR MISSION

Career and Technical Education is a must-have for students and individuals in general across the globe. At WhyCTE, our mission is to foster better understanding of the need for CTE in the realms of both secondary and post-secondary education.

What is our focus?

We as an organization focus on individuals who have been impacted by CTE. This includes anyone who completed their education and moved to a professional job in their chosen field.

We also look for current students who aspire to be practicing professionals, and the teachers of these students. We do our absolute best to provide the most up-to-date forum possible for the raising of new ideas and an effective mechanism for dialog on issues affecting Career Technical Education.
