Who We Are

WhyCTE.us envisions itself as a community of common interest, where individuals can find common ground for communication education.

Founded recently, this organization will be open to anyone in the world who believes in our philosophy and wants to participate.

What is our philosophy?

We believe that it is our duty to advocate for those who have and will participate in CTE programs. We hope to be a beacon of light for individuals in the CTE and STEM communities alike, and promote technical education training and technical jobs in the workforce.
